Current Research / Highlighted Project

Creation of deep learning algorithms for delineation of brain meningioma boundaries in multi-parametric brain MRI scans

My research was done under the mentorship of Dr. Spyridon Bakas and Dr. Ujjwal Baid of the CIBCA at the University of Pennsylvania. We conducted research to create publicly available deep learning algorithms for delineation of brain meningioma boundaries in multi-parametric brain MRI scans using models available through the Generally Nuanced Deep Learning Framework (GaNDLF). Other Notable projects

Neuroability AI

Designed to assist individuals with learning disabilities (dysgraphia and dyslexia) and educators assisting them. Involves a deep neural network trained on the MNIST dataset depoyed to a Flask web application.

New York Times Bestsellers Scraper

A webscraping application focused on the NYT Bestsellers list. Scrapes the various bestselling categories, and within that, datapoints about the first 15 books within the respective category. Converts the list to CSV format.

A* Algorithm Visualizer

This program uses pygame to help visualize the A* Pathfinding Algorithm. The algorithm goes through series of nodes, comparing their calculated scores, and based on that finding the shortest path to the end node. Created with the help of a 'Tech with Tim' tutorial.
